jQuery.Overlay

A plugin for jQuery to create (show/hide) an overlay over any element.

Demo

H1 Overlay, creates a overlay over the H1 at the top.

Document, creates a overlay over the whole document.

Both overlays can be clicked and they will fade away. See ze source for more info.

Usage

Include your jQuery and jquery.overlay files in your source. And go nuts.
